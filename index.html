Rules

Objective: Get as close to 21 as possible, without going over.

  1. Aces may be counted as 1 or 11 points, 2 to 9 according to pip value, and tens and face cards count as ten points.
  2. A player starts with two cards, one of the dealer's cards is hidden until the end.
  3. Hit: A player draws another card
  4. Stand: A player holds their total and ends their turn.
  5. If you go over 21 you bust, and the dealer wins regardless of the dealer's hand.
  6. If you are dealt 21 from the start (Ace and 10), you got a blackjack (Natural BlackJack).
  7. Dealer will hit until his/her cards total 17 or higher.
  8. If a Player and a Dealer both have the same score or a natural blackjack then it is a push.
  9. No split is allowed in my casino:P
